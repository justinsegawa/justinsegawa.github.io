Final Sprint and (11/13/22 to 11/27/22)

Introduction

A little bit of an irregular week, but also less stressful, with Thanksgiving break mixed in.

Final Balancing

After pushing patch notes at the end of last sprint, I spent the week focusing more on how the game felt to play. At team meetings I received feedback that the game still felt too "fast" or overwhelming. Players felt the need to constantly fire and shoot their weapons, with even less downtime than FTL.

As a potential solution with the remaining time that we have, I am experimenting with a universal cooldown time increase for all weapons, and then adjusting specific weapons from there. These changes will be included in the final patch notes of the game.

Wrapping up

Unfortunately the timing for this blog post just barely misses the final week of work on Project: Light, and the Thanksgiving break means a lot less to go over. However, I plan on updating this blog one last time with the final changes contributed the game, as well as closing thoughts and a reflection on the project!
